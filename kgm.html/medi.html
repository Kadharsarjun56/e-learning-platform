Medical Lab

Medical laboratory science (MLS) is the health profession that provides diagnostic services needed to detect and treat disease. Diagnostic services include critical chemical, hematological, immunologic, microscopic, and bacteriological analyses on blood, tissues, and bodily fluids that aid in a patient's overall diagnosis. Clinicians depend on accurate laboratory data to diagnose diseases, while other medical professionals (e.g. nurses and pharmacists) rely on this information to plan or implement treatment and care for their patient.
